Key Changes

Shadow Orbs!
The Shadow Orb system receives a complete overhaul in Mist of Pandaria! Gone are the random procs - now Mind Blast and Shadow Word: Death generate Shadow Orbs reliably. You can store up to 3 orbs, and they're consumed by Devouring Plague for devastating damage. This change makes our rotation more predictable and allows for strategic orb banking during movement phases. Key Changes:
- Shadow Orbs now generate from Mind Blast (guaranteed)
- Shadow Word: Death also generates orbs
- Devouring Plague consumes all stored orbs
- Maximum of 3 orbs can be stored
- Orbs no longer randomly proc from DoT ticks
