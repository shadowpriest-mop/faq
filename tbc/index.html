Gear & Sim

Gearing decisions in TBC are heavily sim-driven. This section covers Best in Slot lists per phase, stat priority order, and a Wowhead overview for quick reference. When in doubt, sim your own character, as small differences in gear can flip priorities.
Best in Slot
Phase 2 BiS
WowSims: Mogu'shan Vaults, Heart of Fear & Terrace of Endless Spring
Phase 3 BiS
WowSims: Throne of Thunder
Stat Weights & Priorities
1
Intellect
Comes naturally on gear, generally just aim for the highest item level available.
2
Hit Cap (15%)
Both Hit and Expertise now contribute to the hit cap for casters. Reach 15% before worrying about other secondary stats.
3
Haste
Will always have high value, even beyond reaching breakpoints.
4
Mastery
With UVLS, this stat is usually going to be your best alternative over critical strike.
5
Critical Strike
With UVLS, when active the value drops to 0. Which means over a fight, the overall gain is reduced.

Secondary stat priorities can shift depending on your current gear setup, always sim your character to confirm.

What is the Analyser?

The Shadow Priest Analyser is a companion tool designed to help you dig through your raid logs and identify areas for improvement. Paste in your Warcraft Logs URL or ID & select your character and fight. Get actionable feedback on your rotation, cooldown usage, and more.

More details and a walkthrough video will be added here once the tool is ready for a wider release. Stay tuned.
